Human Review Workflow

How AI and your judgment work together.

"AI supports the repetitive work. The agent stays in control of the relationship, advice, strategy, and approval."
  1. AI drafts or summarizes.
  2. The agent reviews the output.
  3. The agent edits anything that needs professional judgment.
  4. The agent approves client-facing language before it is sent.
  5. The system logs the task, note, or follow-up.
  6. Anything involving agency, legal interpretation, pricing strategy, compensation, negotiation, or broker supervision stays human.
After the build

What happens once the system goes live.

Every build ships with a clear scope, a short post-launch review, and a path for ongoing oversight if you want it.

Post-launch support

AI systems need review after they meet real business conditions. Most builds include a short post-launch review window. After that, ongoing AI Systems Oversight is available for agents, teams, or brokerages that want continued support, refinement, and workflow monitoring.

Scope boundaries

A workflow build includes one defined process with one primary outcome. Additional workflows, major scope changes, new platforms, or expanded automations are handled as separate phases — so scope, budget, and risk stay clear.
