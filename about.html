About Melissa

Licensed Florida broker. AI implementation strategist.

I help real estate agents, teams, and small brokerages figure out where AI belongs in their business — and design the workflows, guardrails, and implementation plan to make it useful in real life.

Background

Real estate first. AI second.

I'm a licensed Florida broker and licensed real estate instructor with more than a decade in the business — across transactions, operations, training, and team leadership.

I've sat at closing tables. I've taught new agents how representation, agency, and compliance actually work. I've run operations under real broker supervision. That's the lens I bring to AI implementation: real estate first, AI second.

"AI does not replace your judgment. It helps with the repeatable work around your judgment."

Most AI consultants don't have that background. They're selling technology in a regulated industry without understanding the licensing, the supervision duty, or the points in a transaction where human judgment isn't optional.

That's the gap I work in — turning AI from hype into practical business systems for the way real estate work actually happens.
